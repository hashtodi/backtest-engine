# Quarterly Re-Rating Filter — Q3 FY25-26 (quarter ended 31-Dec-2025)

**"Beat the market expectation" screen — Indian mid/small-caps.** Identifies stocks that beat the market's *pre-result* expectation in a way not already priced in (most likely to re-rate up).

| | |
|---|---|
| Analysis date | 2026-06-29 (point-in-time reconstructed as-of D−1 for every expectation input) |
| Universe | 12 board-meeting outcomes, board dates 08-Jan-2026 → 12-Feb-2026 |
| **Analyzed** | **10 of 12** |
| Not analyzed | POONAWALLA, RRKABEL — data run incomplete (see §2) |
| Parameters | PEG_THRESHOLD=1.2 · BEAT_BUFFER=0 · MOMENTUM_WINDOW=1 month · NEAR_ZERO_BASE_GUARD=1000% · basis: consolidated diluted |

**Method.** Two-leg gate per stock. **Leg 1 (beat gate):** vs pre-D analyst consensus where it exists (all three of sales, adj-PAT, margin must beat), else a **proxy** vs the company's own trailing-4Q-average YoY trend (sales & adj-PAT accelerating + YoY margin expansion). **Leg 2 (PEG):** trailing P/E ÷ adjusted YoY EPS growth% must be < 1.2 (loss→profit operational swings pass as *turnarounds*). All ratios use **adjusted/operational** PAT & EPS (exceptionals and abnormal other income stripped). TTM EPS for the trailing P/E is the **four quarters ending Sep-2025** — it excludes the just-reported Dec-2025 quarter.

**Verification.** Each stock was researched once, then an **independent skeptic re-sourced every pre-D figure from a different source** and tried to refute the verdict. For all 10 analyzed stocks the verifier returned **`UNCHANGED` with no look-ahead violations** — i.e. no D-1 price, TTM component, consensus claim, or momentum anchor survived as wrong. (`\*` in tables = a minor verifier rounding correction.)

Note this is a razor-thin failure: the trailing PAT-growth bar is artificially elevated by very low prior-year base quarters (Jun-2024 and Dec-2023 PAT ~Rs 20cr), so on a median or noise-adjusted basis the leg would pass; under the strict mechanical mean rule it does not.
